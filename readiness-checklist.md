# Digital Testing Readiness Checklist

A simple checklist to verify that teams and environments are ready before starting structured testing.

## 1. Roles & Responsibilities
- Test coordinator identified  
- Contact persons in each institution confirmed  
- Channels for communication and escalation agreed  

## 2. Environments & Access
- Test environment available and stable  
- User accounts created and tested  
- Access rights aligned with test scenarios  

## 3. Data & Scenarios
- Test data prepared and anonymised if needed  
- Key scenarios documented  
- Preconditions and expected outcomes clarified  

## 4. Documentation
- Short guidance shared with all participants  
- Reporting template for observations and issues available  
- Timeline and milestones communicated  

## 5. Follow-Up Structure
- Process for issue logging agreed  
- Responsibilities for triage and resolution defined  
- Schedule for follow-up meetings confirmed
